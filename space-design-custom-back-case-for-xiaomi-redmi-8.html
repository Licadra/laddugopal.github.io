Space Design Custom Back Case for Xiaomi Redmi 8

The back cover is compatible with Xiaomi Redmi 8 phone model, which is made by Xiaomi brand.The Mobile case has Space design.You can Design and Customize the Cover with your Pictures or Text. You can also use your Social Media Pictures on the personalized case. Just click on Add Image or Add Text to start designing your own unique case. The case is available in 2 materials – Hard & Soft. Hard Case is made up of Poly Carbonate material with matte finish. Soft Cases are made up of silicone TPU material. Hard Cases are printed on back and both the sides. Soft Cases are printed on back only.

Complete Protection

The case covers 100% of the phone's body from back and both the sides. This offers 360 degree protection to your device on the back and sides and protects it from unintentional drops and other accidents.

Complete Access to Buttons, Ports, Camera & Mic

Precise Cutouts on this cover leave space for Camera, Flash, Buttons, Ports, Microphone & Headphones. The case is completely compatible with Original Accessories like Charger & Earphones.
